Overview

sun-a-wear was a Swiss-based startup from the vision of helping as many people as possible to deal with the effects of UV radiation on the human body and to deal with it more safely. Here, I worked as a designer. In about two months, I have completed several small projects and engaged in the process of sun-a-wear development. I designed digital and printed advertisements across multiple platforms to drive product sales, improved the usability of current application, and assisted in the development of the company's social media design strategy.
