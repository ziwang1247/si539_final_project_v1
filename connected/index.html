CONNECTED

asynchronous Family App

This app offers an asynchronous family communication experience designed to strengthen connections among family members, even when they live apart. Unlike social media platforms like Instagram, it creates a small, private community where users exclusively share their lives with distant family members.

The app features functions such as displaying multiple time zones, showing real-time locations, sending short video messages, and sharing posts. These features are designed to bridge the gap between users and their families, making communication with loved ones across different time zones easier.
